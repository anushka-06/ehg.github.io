Our Story

Our story began with a modest motel in the small town of Grandview, Missouri that currently spans almost three decades in the hospitality industry.

Emerge Hospitality Group was founded with the goal of delivering unforgettable experiences for our guests with the values of taking care of our associates who are the heartbeat that bring the hotel to life. A family-owned and -operated company to this day that owns, develops, and manages brand associated with Hilton Worldwide, Marriott International, and Hyatt Hotels.
